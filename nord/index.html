An influential, welcoming, and active Norden

  • Regional Revival Program

    As the creators of the Regional Revival Program, we believe it is the best way to improve activity and participation in our region.

  • Recruitment telegrams

    We will maintain our current recruitment program, using the Telegram API to send telegrams to new nations 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

  • No regional military force

    Norden is not a military region and has no need for a regional military; we should maintain our neutral status and stay out of R/D.

  • Norden as a role-play region

    We wish to position Norden as primarily a role-play region, and in order to do so, we will focus on improving role-play services and actively updating the map.

  • No political alignment

    Norden must remain open to all political ideologies. We do not wish to add tags to the region such as Socialist or Conservative.

  • Simple citizenship policy

    We support having most citizenship based on WA membership, with the Storting having the authority to grant citizenship to additional nations.

  • Official multilingualism

    We support keeping our two official languages with equal legal status in order to maintain our region's Nordic theme and culture.

  • Parliamentary government

    With a parliamentary system, power is shared more evenly among elected representatives, who are more accountable to the voters.

  • Foreign policy and World Assembly

    Through the Delegacy, we will become more involved in WA politics and build upon our ties with friendly regions, such as the NWA.

First steps in government

  1. Rules of procedure

    Pass the Storting's rules of procedure, establishing a separate, public Discord server for Storting business and allowing for representatives to form specialized committees.

  2. Committees

    Form committees for foreign affairs, role-play, and language/translation.

  3. Statsminister

    Elect a Statsminister to oversee the regional government, along with other regional officers upon the recommendation of the committees.

  4. Basic laws

    Pass laws regarding citizenship, the WA Delegate, the Discord server, and the Regional Message Board.

  5. Role-play

    Re-open map requests and reset the role-play canon (not including the map).
